    Hi, I'm Itziar and I am passionate about Spanish. I come from the city of Pamplona in Spain where I gained experience in teaching both adults and children.     Corporations

    Each company is unique and therefore I customise Spanish lessons for corporates according to the required business situation. I am aware of the time constraints faced by employees, therefore I put emphasis in maximizing the learning potential in the the allotted time. I accomplish this by conducting lessons on-premisis and by creating a dynamic learning environment that is applicable and fun.

    The ultimate goal of learning Spanish in a corporate setting is to be able to better conduct business. These lessons will improve an employees confidence in conducting business in Spanish.

    For more information, please make contact.

    Adults

    Adult classes are customized according to an individual's need. Beginner, intermediate and advanced levels are catered for, with students being encouraged to sit for the DELE exam.

    I also teach special classes for those who want to learn Spanish for their travels. The emphasis is on combining grammar with speaking skills in order to develop basic conversational skills needed for travel.

    Classes can be held in private, or in groups

    For more information, please make contact.

    Children

    Learning a new language at a young age not only improves confidence, it also leads to increased academic performance. I teach children in the following age groups:

    Ages 5-8

    An introduction to basic Spanish adapted to young children. Basic vocabulary and expressions are taught.

    Ages 8-12

    Reading and writing Spanish is introduced. In addition, vocabulary and expressions are taught.

    Ages 12-17

    Thematically designed classes that teach speaking, listening, writing and reading. Students are encouraged to take the A1 level DELE exam for when ready.

    For more information, please make contact.
