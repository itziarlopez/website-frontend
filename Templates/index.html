    Hi, I'm Itziar and I am passionate about Spanish. I come from the city of Pamplona in Spain where I gained experience in teaching both adults and children. I now live in beautiful Vancouver where I have been teaching Spanish to children in kindergarten all the way up to adults who have retired.

    I will help and motivate you to master Spanish! I have been doing this for years and along the way, I have collected the best materials to aide in the Spanish learning process. From conducting business in Spanish to teaching little children, I am excited to be your personal teacher.

    Corporations

    Each company is unique and therefore I customize Spanish lessons for corporations according to the required business situation. I am aware of the time constraints faced by employees, therefore I put emphasis in maximizing the learning potential in the the allotted time. I accomplish this by conducting lessons on-premisis and by creating a dynamic learning environment that is applicable and fun.

    The ultimate goal of learning Spanish in a corporate setting is to be able to better conduct business. These lessons will improve an employees confidence in conducting business in Spanish.

    For more information, please make contact.

    Adults

    Every individual has different needs and so I adapt these lessons to your requirements. I offer beginner, intermediate and advanced classes and I encourage students who are ready to sit for the DELE exam.

    I also teach special classes for those who want to learn Spanish for their travels. The emphasis is on combining grammar with speaking skills in order to develop basic conversational skills needed for travel.

    Classes can be held in private, or in groups

    For more information, please make contact.

    Teenagers

    Learning a second language while being a teenager is always an advantage as at this time of their lives they can really learn and make progress faster than at other ages. Teenagers will learn while having fun with my classes as I often incorporate visuals, music and other games that help them to develop their communication skills.

    Learning a foreign language can also open new opportunities not only for University but for a future career. And also is an excellent tool when travelling as they will be able to interact with other cultures and nationalities.

    For more information, please make contact.

    Children

    Learning a new language at a young age not only improves confidence, it also leads to increased academic performance. This advice applies to children that may seem too young to benefit from learning another language. To keep children interested, I make my lessons fun while still being informative.

    I love teaching children my language. I teach to the following age groups:

    Ages 5-7

    An introduction to basic Spanish adapted to young children. Basic vocabulary and expressions are taught.

    Ages 8-12

    Reading and writing Spanish is introduced. In addition, vocabulary and expressions are taught.
